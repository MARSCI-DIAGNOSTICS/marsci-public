# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: 2026-03-08

## Data Collection

AFX collects and processes KPI data for diagnostic analysis purposes.

### Types of Data Collected

- **KPI Metrics**: Performance indicators you provide
- **Usage Data**: API calls, timestamps, client information
- **Diagnostic Results**: Internal traces and public-safe outputs

### Data Usage

- Diagnostic analysis and pattern detection
- Service improvement and optimization
- Compliance and audit logging

## Data Protection

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Sensitive algorithm details are never exposed
- Audit logs are immutable and tamper-proof
- Data retention follows compliance requirements

### Your Rights

- Access to your personal data
