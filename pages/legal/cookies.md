# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated**: 2026-03-08

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your interactions with AFX.

---

## Types of Cookies Used

### 1. Essential Cookies
- **Purpose**: Required for authentication, security, session management
- **Duration**: Session-based or expires when you close browser
- **Consent**: Required for service to function
- **Examples**: Session tokens, CSRF protection

### 2. Analytics Cookies
- **Purpose**: Understand how users interact with AFX
- **Duration**: Up to 2 years
- **Consent**: Optional - service works without these
- **Tools**: Cloudflare Analytics

### 3. Performance Cookies
- **Purpose**: Optimize service speed and reliability
- **Duration**: Up to 1 year
- **Consent**: Optional
- **Examples**: CDN cache headers, load balancing

---

## Cookie Management

### Enabling/Disabling Cookies

Most browsers allow you to:
- Block all cookies
- Allow only certain types
- Clear cookies automatically on exit

**Note**: Disabling essential cookies may prevent AFX from functioning properly.

### Cookie Settings
- Visit your browser settings
- Search for "Cookies" or "Privacy"
- Adjust cookie preferences

### Third-Party Cookies
- Cloudflare Workers may set cookies
- Third-party analytics providers may use cookies
- You can opt-out via provider settings

## Do Not Track (DNT)

If your browser sends a DNT signal, AFX will:
- Still use essential cookies (required for security)
- Respect your preference for non-essential cookies where possible
- Note that some site functionality may be limited

---

## Cookie Consent

First-time visitors will see a banner requesting consent for non-essential cookies.

Consent is:
- Optional (service works without it)
- Modifiable (you can change preferences anytime)
- Revocable (you can clear cookies and opt-out)
