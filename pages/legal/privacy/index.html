MarSci Diagnostics ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of app.marsci-diagnostics.com.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

01

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the diagnostic service.

Account Information
When you create an account via Google or Facebook OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from that provider. We do not receive or store your social media password.

Ad Platform Data (via OAuth)
To run diagnostics, we request read-only access to your connected advertising accounts (Meta Ads, Google Ads). We collect campaign performance metrics such as impressions, clicks, spend, conversions, CTR, and CPM. We never request write permissions.

Usage Data
We collect anonymized interaction logs — pages visited, diagnostic reports generated, features used — to improve the product experience. This data does not include ad campaign content.

Account info Ad performance metrics Usage analytics
02

How We Use Your Information

  • Run diagnostics — Your ad performance data is processed by our inference engine to detect patterns, anomalies, and optimization opportunities.
  • Generate reports — Diagnostic results are compiled into structured reports delivered within the platform and optionally by email.
  • Improve the product — Aggregated, anonymized usage data is used to improve detection accuracy and develop new features.
  • Customer support — Account and contact information is used to respond to support requests.
  • Billing — Email is shared with Stripe (our payment processor) to manage subscriptions and invoices.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

03

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or ad data to any third party.

We share data only with the following categories of service providers, all bound by confidentiality agreements:

  • Cloudflare — CDN, DNS, and edge compute infrastructure
  • Stripe — Payment processing and subscription management
  • Cloud storage providers — Encrypted data storage (data at rest)

All service providers are contractually required to process data only as instructed by us and may not use your data for their own purposes.

04

Third-Party Integrations

MarSci Diagnostics connects to the following advertising platforms via standard OAuth 2.0 authorization:

  • Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) — Read-only access to campaign insights
  • Google Ads — Read-only access to campaign performance data

Important: We only request read permissions from these platforms. We never write to, modify, pause, or delete your campaigns. You can revoke our access at any time through your Meta or Google account settings.

These platforms have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them: Meta Privacy Policy · Google Privacy Policy

05

Data Retention

  • Active accounts — Data is retained for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to support re-activation or disputes.
  • Account deletion — When you request account deletion, all personal data and ad performance data associated with your account will be permanently purged within 30 days.
  • Billing records — Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Diagnostic logs — Anonymized, aggregated diagnostic records with no personal identifiers may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.
06

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export — Request a structured, machine-readable export of your diagnostic history.
  • Revoke integration access — Disconnect your Meta or Google Ads accounts at any time from the platform settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@marsci-diagnostics.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the service to function:

  • Session cookies — To keep you logged in during your session.
  • CSRF tokens — To protect form submissions against cross-site request forgery.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services.

No cookie consent banner is required because we only use strictly necessary cookies.

08

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Encryption in transit — All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest — Stored data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access control — Access to production systems is limited to authorized personnel and protected by multi-factor authentication.
  • OAuth tokens — Ad platform OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and are never logged in plaintext.
  • Incident response — In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the incident.
