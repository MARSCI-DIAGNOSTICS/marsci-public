# Refund Policy

**Last Updated**: 2026-03-08

---

## Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available within **30 days** of purchase if:
- You have not fully utilized the service
- You experienced a service defect that was not remedied within 5 business days
- Subscription terms were not met

---

## Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds are **not available** for:
- Disputes regarding diagnostic accuracy (diagnoses are recommendations, not guarantees)
- Customer-requested cancellations after 30 days
- Services consumed beyond the refund window
- Custom implementation or consulting services

---

## Refund Process

1. Contact support@marsci-diagnostics.com with refund request and order details
2. Provide explanation and evidence of issue
3. AFX will review within 5 business days
4. If approved, refund will be processed within 10 business days

---

## Billing Disputes

For billing discrepancies:
- Report within 90 days of charge
- Provide transaction details and explanation
- AFX will investigate and respond within 10 business days

---

## Subscription Cancellation

- Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime
- Annual subscriptions may be cancelled with prorated refund
- Cancellation takes effect at end of billing period

---

For refund inquiries, contact: support@marsci-diagnostics.com
