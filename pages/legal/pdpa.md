# PDPA Compliance Notice

**Jurisdiction**: Singapore (Personal Data Protection Act)

**Last Updated**: 2026-03-08

---

## PDPA Requirements

AFX is committed to complying with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) in handling personal data.

---

## Consent

When you use AFX:
- You consent to collection of KPI metrics and usage data
- You consent to storage for diagnostic analysis
- You can withdraw consent at any time

---

## Purpose Limitation

Personal data collected is used only for:
- Providing diagnostic services
- Improving service quality
- Compliance and audit logging
- Fraud prevention

Data will not be used for unrelated purposes without consent.

---

## Data Subject Rights (PDPA Article 21)

You have the right to:

### 1. Right of Access (PDPA 21.1)
- Request copy of personal data held by AFX
- Response within 30 days

### 2. Right to Correct (PDPA 21.1)
- Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- Response within 30 days

### 3. Right to Cease Collection (PDPA 22.1.a)
- Request cessation of collection, use, or disclosure
- Some legal obligations may require continued processing

### 4. Right to Cease Use (PDPA 22.1.b)
- Request cessation of use or disclosure (except legal requirements)
- Some audit obligations may require continued retention

### 5. Right to Opt-Out (PDPA 21.3)
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Effective immediately upon request

---

## Sensitive Personal Data

AFX does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (race, religion, health, etc.).

If such data is inadvertently included in KPI submissions:
- Immediate deletion upon discovery
- Notification to data subject
- Incident documentation

---

## Data Processing Agreement

Organizations subject to PDPA should execute a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with AFX before providing personal data.

---

## Data Breach Notification

In event of data breach:
- Notification to affected individuals within reasonable timeframe
- Notification to Personal Data Protection Authority if serious
- Documentation and remediation

---

## Data Retention

Personal data is retained for:
- **Active Use Period**: Duration of service relationship
- **Audit Period**: 7 years for regulatory compliance
- **Disposal**: Secure deletion after retention period

---

## Third-Party Service Providers

AFX uses service providers (hosting, analytics) who process personal data on AFX's behalf.

All service providers execute Data Processing Agreements ensuring PDPA compliance.
