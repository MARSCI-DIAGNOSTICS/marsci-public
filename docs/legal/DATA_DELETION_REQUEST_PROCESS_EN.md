# Data Deletion Request Process

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This page is a draft process note for MarSci Diagnostics. It explains how users may request access, correction, deletion, or export of personal data, subject to applicable law, technical feasibility, and operational limitations.

## 1. Request Types

Users may contact MarSci Diagnostics regarding:

- access to personal data
- correction of inaccurate data
- deletion requests
- data export requests

## 2. How to Submit a Request

Requests may be submitted to:

- support@marsci-diagnostics.com

Please include enough information for MarSci Diagnostics to identify the account and understand the request.

## 3. Verification Process

Before acting on a request, MarSci Diagnostics may verify the identity and authority of the requester. Additional information may be requested where reasonably necessary to prevent unauthorized disclosure, deletion, or modification.

## 4. Response Timeline

MarSci Diagnostics may respond within a reasonable period, taking into account the type of request, legal requirements, account complexity, and the information available. Exact response timelines are not finalized in this draft and may depend on applicable law.

## 5. Possible Limitations

Certain data may not be deleted, exported, or modified immediately, including where retention is required for:

- legal obligations
- billing and accounting records
- fraud prevention
- security investigation
- audit requirements
- dispute handling
- technical backup or system integrity constraints

## 6. Service Impact

Deletion or restriction requests may affect access to some or all parts of the service where the requested data is necessary to operate the account or provide the service.

## 7. Additional Notes

This process may be updated as MarSci Diagnostics implements additional account controls, export tooling, or deletion workflows.
