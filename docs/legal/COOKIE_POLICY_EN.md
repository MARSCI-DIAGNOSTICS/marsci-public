# Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Status: Prelaunch public legal pack

This Cookie Policy is part of the prelaunch public legal pack for MarSci Diagnostics. It explains the intended public approach to cookies and similar technologies before general release and may be updated as operations, infrastructure, or legal review are finalized.

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website or application. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, or comparable browser-based tools.

## 2. How MarSci Diagnostics May Use Cookies

MarSci Diagnostics may use cookies or similar technologies for:

- essential service operation;
- authentication and login state;
- session management;
- security and abuse prevention; and
- limited service measurement or performance monitoring where implemented.

## 3. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies or equivalent technologies may be used to:

- keep the service running;
- support account sign-in;
- maintain security-related session state; and
- help route requests correctly.

These functions are generally necessary for the service to operate.

## 4. Authentication Cookies

Where browser-based sign-in flows are used, cookies or similar storage may be used to support authentication state, callback handling, and account access continuity.

## 5. Session Management

Session-related cookies or similar storage may be used to:

- keep users signed in where available;
- maintain temporary request state; and
- support basic product navigation and session integrity.

## 6. Usage Measurement

Additional usage-measurement tools may be added later. If used, they may rely on cookies or similar technologies to measure product usage, stability, and performance. The exact tools are not finalized in this draft.

## 7. Cookie Consent

Where required or when available, the website may display a cookie consent banner or similar control. Users may be given the option to accept or decline non-essential cookies. This draft does not claim that such a banner is already implemented.

## 8. Managing Cookies

You may be able to control cookies through your browser or device settings. Blocking some cookies may affect authentication, session continuity, or service functionality.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as product behavior, infrastructure, or legal requirements change.
