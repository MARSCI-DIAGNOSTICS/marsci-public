# Legal Status Decision

## Decision Taken
- Chosen state: prelaunch public legal pack

## Reasoning
- The public legal documents describe intended operating positions ahead of general release.
- Presenting them as final would overstate certainty around operations, billing, infrastructure, and legal review.
- A consistent prelaunch state is more accurate and more conservative for public release.

## Standardized Status Wording
- Primary legal documents now state that they are part of the prelaunch public legal pack for MarSci Diagnostics.
- The wording allows updates as operations, infrastructure, billing, or legal review are finalized.
