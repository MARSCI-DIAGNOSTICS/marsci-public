# Sub-processor Disclosure

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Status: Prelaunch public legal pack

This page is part of the prelaunch public legal pack for MarSci Diagnostics. It identifies service providers that may process data in connection with operating the service before general release. The list may change over time, and this page may be updated accordingly.

## 1. Purpose

MarSci Diagnostics may rely on third-party service providers to support authentication, billing, infrastructure, storage, email delivery, and related operations.

## 2. Current or Expected Sub-processors

| Service Provider | Purpose | Data Type Processed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Google | OAuth authentication; platform integrations where applicable | account identity data, authentication metadata, linked account information |
| Stripe | billing; subscription management | billing references, subscription identifiers, payment status metadata |
| Hosting provider (placeholder) | application hosting; database storage | account data, service data, logs, diagnostic records, operational metadata |
| Email delivery provider (placeholder) | transactional email notifications | email address, message delivery metadata, notification content where applicable |

## 3. Changes to This List

The list of sub-processors may change as MarSci Diagnostics evolves. Updates may be made to this page when service providers are added, replaced, or removed.
